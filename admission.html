Thank you for your interest in Es Shemen International School (ESIS). If you have not already done so, and are in Uganda, we invite you to come and visit our beautiful campus at Plot 9B Dokolo Road at the intersection with Boroboro Road, Senior Quarters B, Lira City east Division.

About Admission

Admission process

ESIS has a “rolling admission” process which means students may enrol at any time during the school year. While we understand that securing school enrolment is a priority for families, we cannot do so until we have received complete applications and reviewed each child’s file for admission. Please visit our school or call +256-777230537 to book an appointment.

Application Fees

Our application fees

To complete an application to ESIS, the application fee of UGX 150,000 per child (Registration, 50,000/=, Application, 50,000/= Diagnostic Test 50,000/=) needs to be deposited to the school's account. Payment may be made by bank transfer or direct deposit to any of the school bank accounts provided below, or though the school business office by cheque.

Grade Placement

Our placement of grades

Students are generally placed in grades based on the age of the child. Please note that we also consider previous school transcripts (where applicable), academic ability, social development, interviews and placement tests (where necessary) to determine grade placement at ESIS.
