Welcome to ESIS

COUNSELING PROGRAM

ESIS's school cousellors deliver a comprehensive, proactive, and developmental guidance program that fosters a safe school environment. In an ethical and confidential manner, school counsellors serve as student advocates, respect individual differnces, and support holistic needs of the diverse range of ESIS's student population by collaborating with school community

Library Service

The mission of the library is to meet the study, teaching, research and outreach information needs for sustainable national and regional and international development needs of the students. The library also boasts of a special collection of information resources, consisting of school archives, manuscripts and photographic materials.
